about

My go to snack anywhere I go whether the city or the beach would be a classic smoothie bowl. I feel smoothie bowls have always been a comfort for me

This was started since I came to Ann Arbor I found the only good place to get Acai was Amers. Although it was good it was not great and something I knew could be even better. I wanted a way to continue to eat smoothie bowls and have an even bigger variety. I began making them and after starting to make them I realized that there was no need to pay 10-15 dollars per small bowl and I could now customize it anyway I want

This website is just a starting point/guide for people who want to start making smoothis bowls on their own but don't know where to begin. I have included recipes that are super simple and recipes that include greens. I know there is a recipe for everyone
